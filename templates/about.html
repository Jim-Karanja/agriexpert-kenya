About AgriExpert Kenya

Empowering Kenyan farmers with intelligent agricultural guidance

Our Mission

To democratize agricultural knowledge by providing smallholder farmers in Kenya with expert recommendations tailored to their specific farming conditions.

Our Vision

A Kenya where every farmer has access to expert agricultural advice, leading to increased food security and improved livelihoods.

How AgriExpert Works

1. Input Your Data

Tell us about your farm conditions, crops, and any problems you're facing.

2. AI Analysis

Our expert system analyzes your information using agricultural best practices and local knowledge.

3. Get Recommendations

Receive personalized, actionable recommendations to improve your farming practices.

Technology Behind AgriExpert

AgriExpert Kenya is powered by CLIPS (C Language Integrated Production System), a sophisticated expert system that encodes agricultural knowledge into a rule-based format.

Key Features:
  • Expert Knowledge Base: Contains tested agricultural practices specific to Kenya
  • Context-Aware Recommendations: Considers altitude, soil type, rainfall, and crop varieties
  • Problem Diagnosis: Identifies pests, diseases, and nutrient deficiencies
  • Priority-Based Advice: Ranks recommendations by urgency and importance
  • Local Adaptation: Tailored for Kenyan farming conditions and crop varieties
